Goal:
    You, the player, will go through different floors of this possessed apartment killing
    the demons summoned by the evil dark magic landlord. After killing a certain number
    of these enemies, you will advance to the next floor of the apartment. On the last
    floor you will encounter the final boss and you must kill them to win the game.

Controls:
    Use WASD or the arrow keys to move
    Use the mouse to aim
    Left-mouse click to attack
    Press Space for dash movement
    Press E near a cat to pet them for small health boost
    Press ESC to pause/unpause
    Press T to pause and view the tutorial
    Press F to toggle display of the FPS counter
    Press R to restart
